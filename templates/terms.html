Privacy Policy

Welcome to the privacy policy of our hospital regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information of patients. Your privacy is important to us.

Collection of Information

We collect various types of information from patients, including but not limited to:

  • Name, contact information, and date of birth
  • Medical history, current medical conditions, and treatment plans
  • Insurance information
  • Information related to hospital visits and appointments
  • Payment and billing information

Use of Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing healthcare services and medical treatment
  • Scheduling appointments and managing hospital visits
  • Processing insurance claims and billing patients
  • Communicating with patients regarding their healthcare
  • Conducting research and analysis to improve patient care

Disclosure of Information

We may disclose personal information in the following circumstances:

  • To healthcare providers and professionals involved in patient care
  • To insurance companies for processing claims
  • To government authorities or law enforcement agencies in response to valid requests or legal obligations
  • For purposes of research and analysis, provided that patient identities remain confidential

Security

We take the security of patients' personal information seriously and implement appropriate measures to safeguard it, including:

  • Access controls and restricted access to sensitive information
  • Encryption of data during transmission
  • Regular monitoring of systems for security vulnerabilities
  • Training staff on privacy policies and security practices

Retention of Information

We retain patient information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this privacy policy or as required by law. When information is no longer needed, we securely dispose of it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update our privacy policy periodically. Any changes will be effective immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page. We encourage patients to review this page regularly for any changes.
