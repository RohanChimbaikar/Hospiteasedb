Vaccines

Elevate your immune defense with our cutting-edge vaccines. Our Vaccine Center is your shield against preventable diseases, offering a range of vaccinations for all ages. From childhood immunizations to travel vaccines, our expert healthcare team ensures your well-being with every shot

X-Ray

Our state-of-the-art X-ray facility is equipped with the latest technology to provide accurate diagnostic imaging. Our team of experienced radiologists ensures high-quality results and quick turnaround times

Physiotherapy

Our dedicated physiotherapy department focuses on enhancing your well-being through personalized rehabilitation programs. Our skilled physiotherapists use advanced techniques to address various musculoskeletal conditions and improve mobility.

Sonography

Our advanced Sonography department is committed to providing accurate and detailed imaging for diagnostic purposes. Our skilled sonographers utilize cutting-edge technology to perform a wide range of ultrasound examinations with precision and care.

Pathology

Our Pathology laboratory is dedicated to providing accurate and timely diagnostic testing services. We employ a team of experienced pathologists and use cutting-edge technology to ensure precise and reliable results for various medical conditions.

MRI

Our MRI facility offers advanced imaging services using state-of-the-art magnetic resonance technology. Our team of skilled radiologists and technicians ensures detailed and high-resolution imaging for accurate diagnosis of various medical conditions.

CT Scan

Our CT Scan facility utilizes cutting-edge technology to provide detailed cross-sectional images for precise diagnostic purposes. Our experienced radiologists and technicians ensure accurate and efficient imaging for a wide range of medical conditions.

Speech Therapy

Our Speech Therapy Department is dedicated to enhancing communication and improving speech-related challenges. Our team of licensed speech-language pathologists works with individuals of all ages, addressing speech and language disorders, articulation difficulties, voice disorders, and swallowing issues.

Cardiology

Our Cardiology department is dedicated to providing comprehensive cardiovascular care. Our team of experienced cardiologists and state-of-the-art equipment ensure accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for various heart-related conditions.

Neurology

Our Neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating a range of neurological disorders, from headaches to complex conditions like epilepsy and stroke. With advanced diagnostic tools and a team of experienced neurologists, we provide personalized care to enhance the well-being of our patients.

Gynaecology

Our Gynecology department is dedicated to providing comprehensive and compassionate care for women's reproductive health. Our team of experienced gynecologists and women's health specialists offers a range of services, including routine check-ups, prenatal care, family planning, and gynecological surgeries.

Emergency

Our Emergency Department stands as a beacon of immediate care, ready to address critical medical needs 24/7. Staffed with a team of dedicated emergency physicians, skilled nurses, and support staff, we are equipped to handle a wide range of medical emergencies.

Intensive Care Unit

Our Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is dedicated to providing critical care for patients with severe or life-threatening conditions. Staffed by a highly skilled team of intensivists, nurses, and healthcare professionals, our ICU is equipped with advanced monitoring and life support systems.

Pediatrics

Our Pediatrics department is dedicated to providing specialized and compassionate healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents. Our team of experienced pediatricians and pediatric nurses is committed to ensuring the well-being and healthy development of young patients.

Gastroenterology

Our Gastroenterology Department is dedicated to the comprehensive care of digestive system disorders and gastrointestinal issues. Led by experienced gastroenterologists and supported by a skilled medical team, we provide a range of diagnostic and therapeutic services.
